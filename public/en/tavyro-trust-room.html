Trust Room

The Executive Intelligence space for the conversations leaders cannot have anywhere else.

Not every strategic question belongs in the company's internal AI environment. Some questions are too sensitive. Too personal. Too consequential.

The Trust Room gives CEOs, executive board members and board directors a dedicated Executive Intelligence space for confidential questions on leadership, organisation and governance — in an independent, protected environment for leadership reflection and strategic decision-making.

This is not ChatGPT.

FAQ

Questions, before you start.

Who is the Trust Room for?+

For CEOs, individual executive board members and board directors who want to reflect on leadership and organisational questions outside the company's internal structures.

How confidential is the Trust Room?+

The Trust Room runs independently of the company's internal AI platform, in its own protected environment. Access is limited exclusively to the individual user personally — CEO, individual executive board member or board director.

Can organisational data be connected?+

Not automatically. The dialogue in the Trust Room remains a separate, independent channel. Those who wish can bring insights from it deliberately and manually into TaVyro Executive Intelligence®, where they are reconciled with HR, ERP and CRM signals.

Is this part of our enterprise AI?+

No. The Trust Room is deliberately designed to be separate from the company's internal enterprise AI environment — for questions that should not be handled there.

What distinguishes Executive Intelligence from an AI assistant?+

An assistant answers questions. The Trust Room structures reflection, challenges assumptions and forms robust hypotheses from them — as a basis for your own decision, not as a ready-made answer.
